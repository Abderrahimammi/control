# Privacy Policy for Gallery Lock: Hide Pictures

**Last updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how "Gallery Lock: Hide Pictures" ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Data You Provide
We do not collect or store any personal information (such as your name, address, or email) on our servers. All personal data, including photos and videos you hide in the Secure Vault, remains stored locally on your device.

### 2. Automatically Collected Data
When you use our App, certain information may be automatically collected by third-party services we use (see "Third-Party Services" below). This may include:
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (Advertising ID).
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App, such as crash logs and performance metrics.
- **Network Information:** IP address, network type (WiFi/Cellular).

## Permissions We Request
To provide the App's core functionality, we request the following permissions:

- **Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES/VIDEO):** Required to access, display, and manage your photos and videos, and to efficiently hide them in the Secure Vault.
- **Camera:** Required for the "Secret Snap" feature (to capture photos of intruders attempting to unlock your vault) and for taking photos directly within the app.
- **Biometric:** Used to authenticate your identity for accessing the Secure Vault using fingerprint or face unlock.
- **Foreground Service:** Used for background tasks such as scanning for duplicate photos or processing large media files.
- **Internet:** Required by third-party ad networks and analytics services.

## Data Security
We take the security of your personal media seriously.
- **Local Storage:** Files hidden in the Secure Vault are stored locally on your device or your SD card. We do not upload your private media to any cloud server.
- **Encryption/Lock:** Access to the Secure Vault is protected by a PIN, Pattern, or Biometric authentication that you set.
- **Caution:** Since data is stored locally, please ensure you back up your data before uninstalling the App or clearing app data to avoid accidental loss.

## Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
